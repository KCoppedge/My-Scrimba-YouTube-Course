Welcome to Medford!

Medford is a small community with some hidden attractions located in Southern Oregon.

Top three activities to do in Medford

Downtown Medford offers quite a wide selection of activities and cuisine, ranging from American bar food to Japanese & Korean. There are hobby stores, clothing and apparel shops that line the streets, all easily accessible with RVTD's transfer station located within.

Why not visit our local outdoor activities such as Table Rock, Roxy Anne or many of the trails around this area? There are too many to choose from, with the nearby town, Jacksonville having over 100 trails to choose from.

If Medford isn't your only stopping place, I recommend seeing Ashland. Roughly twenty-five miles South, Ashland is home to many local shops and resturants with a completely different vibe to Medford.
